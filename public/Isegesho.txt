Mana Data Ushoborabyose,


Ndakwiyambaza uyu munsi n’umutima wicishije bugufi.

Ni Wowe soko y’ubwenge, umugisha n’iterambere ryose.

Mwami, gushyize imbere iyi website yanjye, wambadhishije kuyikora
Uyihe umugisha, uyihindure igikoresho cyiza gifasha abantu, kibigisha, kibahuza kandi kibaha icyizere.

Uyiyobore kugira ngo ikorwe neza, itange serivisi nziza, kandi ibe urumuri ku bayisura bose.

Mana, nyohereza abakoresha benshi bayigana bifuza ubumenyi, impano n’ibyiza iru rubuga rutanga.
Ariko si ukurusura gusa, ahubwo ubahindure abakoresha bahoraho, bayikunda, bayiringira, bayisangiza abandi.

Kuraho inzitizi zose: amakosa, kudakora neza, gutinda cyangwa gucika intege.
Ha website yanjye umutekano, umuvuduko n’ubwiza buhebuje.


Ungire inama mu byemezo byose nyifatira, umpe ubwenge bwo kuyiteza imbere uko bikwiye.


Mwami, ibyiza byose bigaragariremo bibe ikuzo ryawe.

Kandi iterambere ryayo ribe umugisha no kuri njye no ku bandi bose bayigeraho.

Nkigushyize mu biganza byawe, ndabyizeye kandi ndagushimira mbere y’uko mbibona.

Mbisabye mu izina rya Yesu Kristo,
Amen.
